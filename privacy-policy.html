Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email, phone number, and job history when you sign up or apply for jobs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To match you with jobs and employers
  • To communicate about job applications
  • To improve user experience on the platform
  • To send payment or application updates

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information may only be shared with verified employers related to job opportunities you've applied to.

4. Data Security

All user data is stored securely using encrypted Firebase Authentication and Firestore databases.

5. Your Rights

You may update your data or delete your account anytime from your profile page.

6. Updates

This privacy policy may be updated. We’ll notify users of significant changes via email or system message.
