Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for jobs?

Register or log in, then go to "Browse Jobs", click a job, and proceed with the application process.

How much does it cost to apply?

Featured job applications cost KSh 1,150. Interview bookings cost KSh 350. Direct placements cost KSh 950.

Can I edit my profile later?

Yes. Visit the Profile page and update your details including email, phone, or password.

Do I get confirmation after payment?

Yes. Once we verify your payment, a confirmation page and email will be sent automatically.
